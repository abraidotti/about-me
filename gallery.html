Micio is a somewhat lazy cat.

Micio means "kitty" in Italian.

She's can be a wallflower but has understated charisma.

She likes a nice lap when it's cold out.

She sits on things weird.

Micio is not thrilled by clothing and accessories.

She appreciates art.

She likes to hang around people, just not always too close.

And she doesn't mind photo ops.
